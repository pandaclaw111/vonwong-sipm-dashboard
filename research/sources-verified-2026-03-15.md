## 3. Plastics Treaty (INC Negotiations Post-INC-5)

## 5. Pacific Islands ICJ Climate Opinion

**Status:** LANDMARK DELIVERED. The ICJ issued its Advisory Opinion on "Obligations of States in Respect of Climate Change" on **July 23, 2025**. This is a historic ruling affirming states' legal obligations under international law to protect the climate system. Vanuatu led the original UNGA resolution request; Pacific Islands Forum and AOSIS were key advocates. The opinion is non-binding but will be used as legal foundation for climate litigation globally.

**Key Orgs:**

- [Vanuatu government](https://www.gov.vu)

## 10. Deep Sea Mining Moratorium

**Status:** Contested. The International Seabed Authority (ISA) continues processing exploration contracts — Legal and Technical Commission concluded first part of 31st session in March 2026 (confirmed). No commercial mining licenses approved yet. A growing moratorium coalition (30+ countries, including France, Germany, Chile, Fiji, Palau, Canada as of 2025) calls for pause pending environmental baseline studies. Greenpeace running active "Stop Deep Sea Mining" campaign. The Metals Company (TMC) is the most aggressive commercial applicant, seeking to mine Clarion-Clipperton Zone.

## WEF Global Risks Report 2025 — Key Risk Categories
