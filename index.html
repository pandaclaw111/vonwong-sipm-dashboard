The SIPM Framework

The Social Inflection Point Monitor is a framework for reading when movements are ready to tip — and positioning campaigns at those moments.

The Five Conditions for Tipping

Research into past movement inflection points consistently surfaces five conditions. A movement rarely tips on fewer than four. When all five are present, a trigger event ignites a cultural shift.

Condition Definitions

  • Grievance Saturation (0-5): Not just grievance presence — saturation. The harm has accumulated enough that a significant portion feels it personally or viscerally.
  • Narrative Availability (0-5): The explanatory story exists and is accessible — who caused this, who suffers, and what can be done.
  • Organizational Infrastructure (0-5): Networks of people capable of activating are already connected. You can't build this during a crisis.
  • Permission Structure (0-5): The cultural/political environment endorses the movement's values or has become fragile enough to challenge.
  • Trigger Event Potential (0-5): Specific, emotionally legible incidents that prove the grievance is happening right now, to real people.

WEF Risk Categories

This dashboard organizes movements by the World Economic Forum's 5 Global Risk categories to reveal cross-domain connections:

  • 🔴 Economic: Labor, cost of living, debt, gig economy, AI displacement
  • 🟢 Environmental: Climate, plastics, water, extraction, biodiversity
  • 🟠 Geopolitical: War, sanctions, authoritarianism export, state conflict
  • 🟡 Societal: Democracy, inequality, health, migration, polarization
  • 🔵 Technological: AI governance, surveillance, digital rights, deepfakes

Outcome Scoring

  • 5 – Full Change: Durable policy/institutional change achieved and maintained
  • 4 – Major Win: Significant policy wins but vulnerable to reversal or incomplete
  • 3 – Cultural Shift: Permanent change in public consciousness, discourse, or norms
  • 2 – Partial Win: Some concessions extracted but core grievance unaddressed
  • 1 – Awareness Only: Public attention generated without structural change
  • 0 – Suppressed: Movement crushed with no lasting impact

Key Insight

The trigger doesn't create the conditions — the conditions determine when the trigger ignites. Build conditions relentlessly; be ready to amplify when the trigger comes.
